Welcome to Paul Jeggels Designs

Crafting Waves, Crafting Dreams

Founded by master surfer and master shaper Paul Jeggels, Paul Jeggels Designs is more than just a surfboard manufacturing company – it's a labor of love. With decades of experience in the industry, Paul has honed his craft and perfected the art of shaping surfboards that embody the essence of surfing.
